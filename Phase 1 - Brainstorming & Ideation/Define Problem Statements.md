### Customer Problem Statement Template

Create a problem statement to understand your customer's point of view. The Customer Problem Statement template helps you focus on what matters to create experiences people will love.

A well-articulated customer problem statement allows you and your team to find the ideal solution for the challenges your customers face. Throughout the process, you'll also be able to empathize with your customers, which helps you better understand how they perceive your product or service.

### Problem Statements

| Problem Statement (PS) | I am (Customer) | I'm trying to | But | Because | Which makes me feel |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **PS-1** | An online shopper looking for electronics and fashion products | Find and purchase quality products from a reliable online store quickly and conveniently | I often face cluttered product listings, unreliable payment gateways, and confusing navigation on many e-commerce websites | Most platforms lack proper category filtering, secure checkout, and a clean user interface | Frustrated and hesitant to complete my purchase, leading me to abandon my shopping cart |
| **PS-2** | A small business administrator trying to manage an online storefront | Manage product inventory, track customer orders, and monitor overall sales performance from a single dashboard | I have to use multiple disconnected tools for product management, order tracking, and user administration | Existing solutions are either too expensive, overly complex, or don't offer integrated admin dashboards with real-time metrics | Overwhelmed and inefficient, as I spend more time on administration than growing my business |
| **PS-3** | A first-time online buyer who is not tech-savvy | Create an account, browse categories, add items to cart, and make a secure payment seamlessly | I find the registration process complicated and worry about the security of my payment information | Many platforms have lengthy sign-up forms, lack clear category navigation, and don't show transparent pricing (tax, shipping) at checkout | Anxious and distrustful, resulting in me avoiding online purchases altogether |
| **PS-4** | A returning customer wanting to track previous orders | View my order history, check the delivery status of my current orders, and cancel orders if needed | I cannot find a centralized place to view all my past transactions and their statuses | Order management features are often buried in settings or require contacting customer support | Disconnected from my shopping experience and uncertain about the reliability of the platform |
