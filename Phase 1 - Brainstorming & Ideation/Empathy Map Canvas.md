### Empathy Map Canvas

An empathy map is a simple, easy-to-digest visual that captures knowledge about a user's behaviours and attitudes.

It is a useful tool to helps teams better understand their users. Creating an effective solution requires understanding the true problem and the person who is experiencing it. The exercise of creating the map helps participants consider things from the user's perspective along with his or her goals and challenges.

**Reference:** [https://www.mural.co/templates/empathy-map-canvas](https://www.mural.co/templates/empathy-map-canvas)

## Empathy Map 1: Online Shopper (End Customer)

### User Persona
| Attribute | Details |
|:---|:---|
| **Name** | Ravi Kumar |
| **Age** | 25 years |
| **Occupation** | Software Developer |
| **Location** | Hyderabad, India |
| **Tech Proficiency** | Moderate to High |

### SAYS
- "I want to quickly find the product I'm looking for without browsing through hundreds of irrelevant items."
- "I prefer platforms that show transparent pricing — I hate hidden charges at checkout."
- "I need a secure payment method. I don't trust websites that look unprofessional."
- "I wish I could track all my orders from one single page."
- "Category-based filtering saves me a lot of time when shopping."

### THINKS
- "Will my credit card information be safe on this platform?"
- "This website looks clean and modern — it might be trustworthy."
- "I hope the product quality matches what's shown in the images."
- "How long will the delivery take? Can I cancel if needed?"
- "I wish more platforms had this simple, no-clutter approach."

### DOES
- Searches for products using keywords in the search bar
- Filters products by category (Electronics, Fashion, Mobiles, etc.)
- Adds multiple items to the cart and reviews pricing before checkout
- Compares prices with competitor websites
- Checks order history from the profile page
- Registers with email/password for a personalized experience

### FEELS
- **Frustrated** when product search returns irrelevant results
- **Anxious** about payment security on unknown platforms
- **Relieved** when checkout shows clear breakdown of subtotal, tax, and shipping
- **Satisfied** when the admin-seeded catalog has quality, diverse products
- **Empowered** when able to cancel orders directly from the profile
- **Delighted** by a clean, responsive UI with good categorization

### Pain Points
1. Overwhelming product listings without proper filtering
2. Lack of transparent pricing (hidden fees at checkout)
3. Insecure payment gateways with no trust signals
4. Inability to track orders or view purchase history
5. Complicated registration process

### Goals
1. Find and purchase desired products in under 5 minutes
2. Complete payment securely with confidence
3. Track order status and cancel if necessary
4. Browse by category to discover new products
5. Have a personalized account with order history

## Empathy Map 2: Store Administrator

### User Persona
| Attribute | Details |
|:---|:---|
| **Name** | Priya Sharma |
| **Age** | 32 years |
| **Occupation** | Small Business Owner |
| **Location** | Bangalore, India |
| **Tech Proficiency** | Moderate |

### SAYS
- "I need a simple dashboard where I can see how my store is performing at a glance."
- "Adding new products should be as easy as filling out a form — no coding required."
- "I want to manage my product categories and keep the storefront organized."
- "I need to see all customer orders and update their status quickly."
- "Managing users and seeing who's registered helps me understand my customer base."

### THINKS
- "Is my store's data secure? Who has access to admin functionalities?"
- "How can I increase sales? I need better metrics to understand trends."
- "I should be able to update product details without taking the site offline."
- "Managing inventory across categories should be streamlined."
- "I hope the admin panel doesn't require training to use."

### DOES
- Logs into the admin dashboard to check daily sales, order count, and user metrics
- Creates, updates, and deletes products from the product management section
- Adds new categories with images to organize the product catalog
- Reviews customer orders and updates their status (Pending → Shipped → Delivered)
- Monitors registered user accounts and removes spam/fake accounts

### FEELS
- **Overwhelmed** by platforms with complex admin interfaces
- **Confident** when the dashboard shows clear, real-time metrics (total sales, orders, users)
- **Efficient** when product CRUD operations are quick and intuitive
- **Concerned** about unauthorized access to admin functionalities
- **Satisfied** when order management is centralized in one view

### Pain Points
1. No centralized dashboard for key business metrics
2. Complex product management requiring technical knowledge
3. Difficulty tracking and updating order statuses
4. Poor user management capabilities
5. Lack of role-based security (anyone accessing admin features)

### Goals
1. Monitor business performance through a clean admin dashboard
2. Manage product catalog with easy CRUD operations
3. Process and track all customer orders efficiently
4. Maintain a secure, role-protected admin environment
5. Grow the customer base with insights from user data
